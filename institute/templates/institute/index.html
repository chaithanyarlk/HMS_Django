Hostel Management System

Hostel Management System is the one stop solution to all the hostel related activities in National Institute Of Technology, Andhra Pradesh which aims to provide fast, reliable, efficient and optmized solution. The system provides good mechanism to tackle various problems starting from hostel room allotment to attendance management. The hostel management system is capable of handling tedious tasks such as monitoring students, staff and workers in the campus. The system is so smooth and efficient that a person can use it from hand held device itself!.
